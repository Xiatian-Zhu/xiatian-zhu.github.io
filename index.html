    Xiatian Zhu

Xiatian Zhu is a Senior Lecturer at the Surrey Institute for People-Centred Artificial Intelligence, and Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing (CVSSP), Faculty of Engineering and Physical Sciences, University of Surrey, Guildford, UK. Proir to that, he was a research scientist at Samsung AI Centre, Cambridge, UK. He received his Ph.D. from the Queen Mary University of London. His research interests include computer vision and machine learning.
